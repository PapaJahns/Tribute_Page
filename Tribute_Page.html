Quentin Tarantino

  • 1992- Tarantino writes/directs his first film, Reservoir Dogs
  • 1994- Tarantino releases his breakthrough film, Pulp Fiction, and gains instant recognition as well as an academy award for most original screeplay
  • 1997- Releases his third film, Jackie Brown, which is based on the novel, Rum Punch, by Elmore Leonard
  • 2003-2004- Kill Bill series, a highly stylized revenge tale, is released throughout these years. Tarantino's first films which include in-depth choreography and fight scenes. First high-budget films
  • 2009- Quentin releases his sixth film, Inglorious Basterds, which recieves critical acclaim and hit number 1 in the box office
  • 2012- Django Unchained is released, a blaxploitation film about a souther slave achieving his freedom. Becomes Tarantino's highest grossing film and beats Inglorious Basterds from two years prior
  • 2015- Tarantino's eighth film, The Hateful Eight, released on both standard film as well as 70mm
  • 2017-2018- A ninth film called Once Upon a Time in Hollywood is announced. The film will be released in 2019 and will be about the Manson murders
